Queens of the Stone Age - Era Vulgaris

Star Rating: 4/5 stars

Although some fans see this album as a decline from the previous two albums, Era Vulgaris proves that its worthy to be up there with Queens of the Stone Age's other albums. This album has some of the best qotsa singles including "3's & 7's" and "Make it wit Chu". Recommended for fans of rock music


Foo Fighters - Concrete and Gold

Star Rating: 4/5 stars

The newest album released by the very popular band Foo Fighters was well received by fans when it was released. Although it is not as goood as their previous work, its nonetheless a very good album with many enjoyable songs on it such as "Run" and "The Sky is the Neighbourhood". Very much would recommend this to Fans and people who tend to listen to rock music often


Tyler, The Creator -Flower Boy

Star Rating: 4/5 stars

Flower Boy will be without a doubt be regraded as one of the best rap albums and some of Tyler, The Creator's best work the rapper has ever done.
